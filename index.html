My platforms

ThingstodoinSalem.com

Things To Do In Salem is a project 10+ years in the making. After wandering the gorgeous uneven brick streets for as many years as I have, the recommendations, travel tips, event info and more began to accumulate. I decided it was time to try to help others see Salem the way I do, to help others plan a successful trip into a wonderful city. This website came from that desire.
Are you a Salem, MA or North Shore business owner? Click to read about working with ThingstodoinSalem.com.

SalemMAWedding.com

I created SalemMAWedding.com as the sister site to ThingstodoinSalem.com because I receive questions about getting married in Salem regularly. I wanted to combine my knowledge of Salem and background in wedding photography into one place. SalemMAWedding.com offers planning tips, information, vendor and venue highlights as well as a Salem focused wedding planning guide.
Are you a Salem, MA or north shore wedding professional? Click to read about working with SalemMAWedding.com.

PhotographybyThingstodoinSalem.com

In addition to running ThingstodoinSalem.com and SalemMAWedding.com, I am also a professional photographer. I have photographed 100+ weddings, had my work featured on platforms such as Martha Stewart Weddings, Wedding Wire, The Knot, OffBeat Wed and more. I am also a featured vendor with OffBeat Wed. I offer wedding photography, engagement shoots, proposal photography, family photoshoots, business photography and more.
Please send any inquiries to info@thingstodoinsalem.com.
