Social media

I would love to chat with you about the ways we can work together to grow your business and platform. Feel free to contact me if you are looking for something not listed below. I would be happy to discuss your businesses’ needs as each is unique.

Social media is one of the biggest tools available to help business owners grow and reach new people every single day. Without a strong and active online presence, you’re missing out on an amazing resource that puts your information (literally) into the hands of those searching for you.

Maybe you’re new to the social media game and aren’t even sure where to start. Maybe the discussion of algorithms and engagement rates makes your eyes go crossed a little. I would love to help with that.

Currently I offer the following social media services:

01

Social media management

Let me take social media off your plate completely! I’d be happy to get my hands on your account and take care of that while you’re busy running your business.

02

Social media 101

Is your social media account in the newborn phase and you aren’t sure what you’re doing? I’d be happy to sit down with you and explain how that platform works, starting with the basics.

03

Social media audits

Are you already set up online but aren’t sure if you’re doing it right? I’d love to take a look under the hood and see how you have everything running. I am happy to give your account(s) a review and offer my suggestions for growth.

04

Social media clean ups

This service applies only to Pinterest. Pinterest has so much to offer and if you aren’t using it to drive traffic, you’re missing out. I’d be happy to get my hands on your account and clean it up for you. This means I’d be adding branded cover images to your boards, hashtags, keywords and more. I would also be willing to extend this into setting your Pinterest account up with Tailwind so you can use this scheduling tool. (Please note this is at an additional charge, you would need to purchase a yearly subscription to Tailwind.)
