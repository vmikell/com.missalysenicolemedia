Blogging

My heart will forever be first and foremost in content creation. I started as a blogger way back in the day when I was teaching myself HTML basics and it has stuck with me since. I’d love to help you with your businesses’ blogging and content creation needs.

Businesses in the following industries are a particularly good fit for my blogging services: Weddings, travel and tourism, Paganism and New Age spirituality, photography and the arts in general.

This list is not all inclusive and if you aren’t one of those categories, I’d still be happy to talk. I offer packages that are geared towards shorter term projects (ie: a handful of blog posts) all of the way to long term blog post and social media combos, delivered on a monthly schedule.

Newsletter management

Newsletters convert higher than any other tactic because it’s one of the easiest way to reach your customers. They don’t have to come looking for you or seek you out – you’re delivering your news and information right to them. If you don’t currently have a newsletter, you’re missing out! I’d be happy to get you set up and running or take it over for you completely and write your newsletter content for you.
