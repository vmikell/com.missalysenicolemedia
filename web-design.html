Websites and design

I would love to chat with you about the ways we can work together to grow your business and platform. Feel free to contact me if you are looking for something not listed below. I would be happy to discuss your businesses’ needs as each is unique.

Having a strong web site goes hand in hand with a social media presence. You need a site that your potential audience can find, easily navigate and that ranks in the most commonly used search engines.

Currently I offer the following services:

Website audits

If you already have a site and would just like me to take a look, I’d be happy to review it. I would then offer my suggestions for growth or improvement. This audit includes website functionality, design, user experience and SEO.

WordPress.org website set ups

If you don’t have a website at all, I would love to help get you going. This would include help registering a domain name, setting up WordPress.org and getting you up and running.

WordPress.org website set up + Design

If you’d like a website set up plus design, this option is for you. This would include help registering a domain name and setting up WordPress.org. I’d then also design a simple website for you with a few basic sub pages to get you going.
